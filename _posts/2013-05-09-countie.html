Countie is a very minimalistic and lightweight theme; it only loads 2 images (that’s not counting the demo sample content).   • Twitter Feed

    Encourage visitors to follow and interact with you by streaming your own tweets, or query tweets using Twitter’s Search Operators.

    Crafted to provide easy reading and navigation with minimal resizing, panning, and scrolling across a wide range of devices.

    Animated LIVE Countdown that serves to count down in seconds, minutes, hours and days to any specific date.
